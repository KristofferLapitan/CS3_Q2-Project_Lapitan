Introduction
This garden here is a beautiful and peaceful place where I usually stay when I want to relax and appreciate nature.


Plant-filled Japanese Gate
What is there to see?
The garden is filled to the brim with plants. It's diverse, unique, and enchanting as you walk every step. There are different plants from the plainest of grass to the most beautiful of roses.
Keep in mind that not everything is perfect here. My cats and dogs often snap the stems of tall plants and there are some invasive species of plants in our gardern.
Furthermore, our garden is very difficult to maintain and there are some spots where it is quite odorful because of poop or leftovers.
In the end, I still love the garden's environment and scenery. I'm just here to share it with you. :)
