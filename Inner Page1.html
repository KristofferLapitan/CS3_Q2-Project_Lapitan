Cat Family
Who are the Cats in the Garden?

Bangus

He is the eldest brother who usually stays on the kitchen window meowing for food. He always sleeps or meows for food which makes him a very peaceful and calm cat.

Diona

She is the one of the twins who is often playful and her name is inspired by her design which similar to a character from Genshin Impact. She is currently a mother with 3 kids but as of today, only one survived.

Tila-Pia

She is the other twin who is very introverted. However, she is the most playful and energetic cat. She also often hunts for birds or rats found in the garden.

Gigi

She is the mother cat with a hot temper. She often fights her children and leaves the garden to search for food. I rarely see her but when I do she causes trouble most of the time.

Macky Mackerel

He is the youngest sibling and he is very athletic for a kitten. He can climb trees and cages effortlessly. However, just like his sister Tila, he is introverted so I rarely interact with him.

George and Fred

These 2 were formerly triplets but the other died early. Furthermore, Fred died from malnutrition later one. The only child of Diona, George, is a fierce kitten that managed to show dominance over my 4 dogs. He is courageous for such a small creature.
